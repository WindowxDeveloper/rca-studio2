VIP Color Kaleidoscope

Written by Steve Houk, 1978

This program uses the CHIP8X Interpreter at 0000-02FF.
Keys 2, 4, 6 and 8 program patterns for up to 125 entries.
until the Kaleidoscope automatically starts. 
You may begin execution of the Kaleidoscope any time 
during the programming sequence by pressing key 0.
The colors are randomly selected by the game.
