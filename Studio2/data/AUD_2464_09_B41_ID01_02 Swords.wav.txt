RCA Coin Arcade Swords Game

Written by Joe Weisbecker, 1975

Press 25 cent button to start play.

Press A center button for 1 player 
against the computer.

Press B center button for 2 player game

Center button moves sword: low, level, high
Arrow keys move your sword fighter up, down, left, right

Source: Hagley Museum and Library
Wilmington, Delaware
